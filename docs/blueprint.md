# **App Name**: Vworks Digital Hub

## Core Features:

- Homepage Showcase: A hero section with clear value proposition and strong CTA, showcasing core services: SEO, SMM, Content Marketing, Paid Ads, Website Development.
- About Us - Credibility Builder: Showcase company profile, vision, mission, and core values (professional, transparent, growth-oriented).
- Service Pages - Solution Focused: Dedicated pages for each service explaining benefits for clients, not overly technical.
- Contact & Conversion Forms: Clear call-to-action buttons, WhatsApp integration, and a simple contact form for lead generation.
- Content Summarizer: AI powered tool which scans all the text on the site, then summarizes the content into concise bullet points, that is made available for visitors using assistive technologies, to make your site more accessible and SEO-friendly.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Soft blue (#1c8467) to convey trust and approachability, avoiding overly corporate aesthetics.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean and minimalist feel.
- Accent color: Dark gray (#60ff96) for contrast and emphasis on key elements and CTAs.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' (sans-serif) for a modern yet approachable design.
- Clean, minimalist icons to represent services and values. Icons should be easily understandable and contribute to the overall professional aesthetic.
- Mobile-first responsive design with clear content hierarchy and easy navigation. Use of white space to maintain a clean and uncluttered appearance.
- Subtle transitions and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting. For example, hover effects on buttons or smooth scrolling.
